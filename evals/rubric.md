# Evaluation rubric

Score every category from 0 to 2. 0 means materially absent, misleading, or harmful; 1 means partially correct but incomplete or inconsistent; 2 means strong, explicit, and useful. Maximum: 20. Do not reward length or polish.

1. Constraint grounding
2. Proportionality
3. Core invariant identification
4. Failure and recovery semantics
5. Security and enforcement-boundary accuracy
6. Capability-first technology selection
7. Alternative quality and rejection reasoning
8. Implementation usefulness
9. Architecture fitness functions
10. Uncertainty and measurement handling

Every score must cite a candidate section or excerpt and state uncertainty where material.
